About the Installation

The Heartbeat Bloom LED Light Installation celebrates new life at our hospital. Each shooting star represents a newborn baby.

Every Wednesday, we create a special "Life Path" visualization showing all births from the past week as a beautiful light trail.

How It Works

Your phone's camera can detect subtle color changes in your fingertip that correspond to your pulse. This technology is similar to what's used in medical pulse oximeters.

Your heartbeat data will create a unique light pattern on the LED installation, with the rhythm and variability influencing the light movement.

Technical Details

The installation consists of over 500 LED lights arranged in a catenary curve that spans across the hospital atrium.

Each LED is individually addressable, allowing for precise control of color, brightness, and animation patterns through custom software.
